Download from your Audible library

Pull straight from your Audible into a mounted folder. Files are decrypted to standard M4B with their chapters and metadata intact. No metadata fixing needed. Books you already own are flagged so you don't re-download by accident.

Heads up - KNOWN ISSUE - downloads can currently fail due to a known, temporary issue with Amazon's activation API. This is on Amazon's side and typically resolves on its own after a while. If a download fails, wait a bit and try again.

Downloaded books land here, one folder per book. Must be accessible from inside the container.

    After downloading, Folder Forge previews the planned moves and applies them automatically into your destination root.

    Owned This title is already in your library. Selecting it downloads another copy.

    Owned detection comes from Audiobookshelf: a title is flagged Owned when its ASIN is already in your Audiobookshelf library. Fixing a book's ASIN tag is only the first step. Audiobookshelf has to re-scan the file and pick up the new ASIN before the title shows as Owned here, so a freshly fixed book can stay un-owned until Audiobookshelf re-indexes it. Books that Audiobookshelf has not indexed, or that are missing or have an incorrect ASIN, will not be flagged. Run the Metadata Forge to fix ASINs, let Audiobookshelf re-scan, then refresh. (If Audiobookshelf is not connected, owned detection falls back to reading ASIN tags directly from your library files.)
